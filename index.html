About Me

Born in the Philippines and raised in a culturally-diverse city just outside of Seattle, I've always been interested in the bigger picture and the intersections of different worldviews in shaping the future. Prior to starting Epicodus, I had no knowledge and experience in the tech field and knew nothing about coding and programming. Awareness of coding and programming as a form of future literacy, I decided I wanted to develop the knowledge and skill. Epicodus's philosophy of making learning coding accessible to anyone, and the collaborative approach to learning drew me to the program. I hope to find a way to join a startup interested in the merging IT applications with social and environmental causes.
